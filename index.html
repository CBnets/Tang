Short Biography

Dr. Jinglei Tang received her PhD from Northwest A&F University (NWSUAF), Shaanxi,China, in 2010. She is currently associate professor at College of Information Engineering (CIE), Northwest A&F University (NWSUAF). Her research interests include decision support, information management, computer vision, pattern recognition, machine learning, image analysis, etc. She is reviewer for Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) proposals, and reviewer for a lot of journals such as Computers and Electronics in Agriculture, etc. She also is TPC member for a lot of International conferences. Dr. Tang have published more than 20 papers in recently five years, including 6 papers in the international journal of Science Citation Index(SCI).

Courses

Information analysis and decision support, Artificial intelligence and expert system, and C programming.
